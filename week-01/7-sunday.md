### Just updates on what I did.
- Went through the paper [UniSafe](https://arxiv.org/html/2603.17476v1)
- Basically it is a benchmark that has about 6k tests ranging in 7 different tasks ranging from T2T, T2I, IT2T; etc.
- Shows how closed and open models differ in their performance on such benchmarks.
- It shows that even the models which are better then their previous versions, are NOT SAFE. For example, Show-o2 was much better than show-o, but safety wise it was much worse than its previous one. GPT-5 showed the best results, though claude wasn't compared but gemini was much worse than GPT-5.
